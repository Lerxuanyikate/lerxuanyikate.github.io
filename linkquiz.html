Maker's Quest

The Legend of Zelda is a popular game franchise, and its games usually have Link the hero as the protaganist.

This quiz is to test the knowledge of the average Zelda fan on how well they can tell which game each Link belongs to.

Features

  • Self-marking Quiz
  • Simple and engaging
  • A streak counter for every day that all tasks are successfully completed
