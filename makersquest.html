Maker's Quest

A mobile wireframe made in Figma of a task manager application designed for users who want to keep track of their gaming schedule, especially when they play multiple games.

The goal of this product is to provide an easy way for users to track their games’ everyday tasks. This will allow gamers to manage their time efficiently and enjoy their games without fearing missing in-game events or rewards.

Features

  • Toggle-on reminders
  • Progress tracking
  • A streak counter for every day that all tasks are successfully completed
